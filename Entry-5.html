Marinated Beef
Ingredients
  • 2 beef tendons
  • Marinade: star anise, cinnamon, star anise, coriander, peppercorns, scallions, ginger, rock sugar.
  • tablespoons of dark soy sauce
  • Two tablespoons of soy sauce
  • Two tablespoons of soybean paste

    • 1 Bought the tendon cold water with a spoonful of salt soaked for an hour

    2 From the salted water after the tendon in cold water to boil skimming blood foam

    3 Pour in the sauce and marinade and bring to a boil over high heat, then turn to medium heat and simmer for 2.5 hours.

    4 Turn off the heat and simmer for two hours, then serve when cool.

    5 Refrigerate overnight, it is good for cutting, thin and flavorful.
