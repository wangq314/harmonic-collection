Stir Fried Pork with Chili Peppers

Ingredient

  • Two green chili peppers
  • 250g Pancetta
  • 10g garlic
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1/3 tsp sugar

Step 1

    Cut the pork into 0.5cm slices, cut the chili peppers to remove the seeds and cut into bite-sized pieces, set aside.

Step 2

    Heat the oil in a wok and fry the sliced pork until golden brown on both sides.

Step 3

    Add the garlic, one tablespoon of soy sauce, one tablespoon of dark soy sauce and a quarter of a tablespoon of sugar, stir fry well.

Step 4

    Add the chili peppers, stir fry evenly, when the chili peppers soften a little, serve the dish on a plate.
