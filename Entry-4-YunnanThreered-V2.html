200g ground pork
1 Tomato
2 Bell Pepper
2 Red bell pepper
1/3 Garlic
1/3 spoon grated Ginger
1 spoon Soy sauce
1 spoon Oyster sauce
1 spoon Cooking wine
Chop the tomatoes, green and red peppers, garlic and ginger (3:1), and set aside.
Stir fry the ginger and garlic in hot oil, then pour in the pork and stir fry until browned, then add soy sauce and cooking wine to taste, then set aside
Stir fry the tomatoes in the remaining oil! Stir fry the tomatoes to get out the juice, and then pour in the green and red bell peppers and stir fry together.
Pour in the pork and stir-fry, season with a spoonful of soy sauce and a spoonful of oyster sauce.
