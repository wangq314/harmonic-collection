KIMCHI-JJIGEA

Ingredients 180g skinless pork belly, cut into bite size pieces 1 Zucchini 250g kimchi, cut into bite size pieces 1 onion 2 green onion, thinly sliced 150g soft tofu, sliced into 3x3cm 1 Tsp Deojong 1 tsp gochujang 1/2 garlic

Step 1: Add dried anchovies, two slices of dried kelp and 500ml of water to the pot, cook for 10 minutes and then leave to stand. This is the stock that will be used later.

Step 2:While the stock is cooking, add oil to the pot, fry the pancetta until golden on both sides, add the minced garlic, add a spoonful of kochujong and a spoonful of doenjang and kimchi and stir fry.

Step 3: While the stock is cooking, add oil to the pot, fry the pancetta until golden on both sides, add the minced garlic, add a spoonful of kochujong and a spoonful of doenjang and kimchi and stir fry.

Step 4: Add the cooked stock to the pot, add the chopped zucchini, onion and pancetta and cook for 15 minutes, then add the tofu.
Step 5: Serve stew in bowls, drizzle with sesame oil and sesame seeds and top with chopped green onions.
