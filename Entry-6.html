TOMATO STEW

Ingredients 1/2 whole garlic Two slices of ginger 1 potato 1 carrot 2 tomatoes 1 coriander 300g beef (tendon/brisket) 2 tablespoons Pixian bean paste 1 tbsp soy sauce

Step 1: Prepare a pot of hot water. Cut the beef, tomatoes, carrots and potatoes into almost equal sizes. Soak the beef in cold water for 20 minutes to clean the blood.

Step 2:Pour the oil into the pot and when the oil is hot, add the chopped beef cubes and ginger slices. Fry the beef cubes until lightly browned. Then add a tablespoon of Pixian bean paste and stir fry evenly.

Step 3: Add the pre-boiled hot water to the pot and simmer the tomatoes, carrots and garlic for 15 minutes, then add the potatoes and a spoonful of soy sauce and simmer for another 15 minutes. While you wait chop the parsley into 1cm wide sizes

Step 4: Serve the stew in a bowl and top with a good amount of parsley.
