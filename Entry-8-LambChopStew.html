500G LAMB CHOPS
2 CARROTS
1/2 GREEN ONION
3 SPLICE GINGER
1/2 TSP WHITE PEPPER
1/3 TABLESPOON CORIANDER SEEDS
1/3 TSP PEPPER
1 PIECE OF CINNAMON
2 TPS DRIED CHILI PEPPER
2 TPS OF BEAN PASTE
1 TPS DRIED SHRIMP
1 TPS LIGHT SOY SAUCE
01
BLANCH THE LAMB CHOPS IN COLD WATER, REMOVE THE SURFACE FOAM WHEN THE WATER BOILS, AND THEN REMOVE THE LAMB CHOPS FROM THE POT.
02
PUT OIL IN THE POT, WHEN THE OIL IS HOT, ADD 2 TABLESPOONS OF SUGAR, SLIGHTLY DISCOLORED AND ADJUST THE MINIMUM FIRE TO START STIRRING, STIR-FRY DATE-RED SUGAR COLOR INTO THE MUTTON CONTINUE TO STIR-FRY
03
ADD GREEN ONIONS, GINGER, WHITE PEPPER, CORIANDER SEEDS, PEPPER, CINNAMON, A HANDFUL OF DRIED CHILI PEPPERS, A TABLESPOON OF BEAN PASTE, STIR-FRY UNTIL EVEN
04
ADD HOT WATER OVER LAMB, ADD DRIED SHRIMP, SOY SAUCE AND SIMMER FOR ONE AND A HALF HOURS ON LOW HEAT
05
AFTER THE LAMB IS SOFT AND ROTTEN, ADD CARROTS, TEN MINUTES AFTER THE FIRE JUICE
