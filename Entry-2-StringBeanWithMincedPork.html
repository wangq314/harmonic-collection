250G STRING BEANS
250G MINCED PORK
2 SOY SAUCE
1 OYSTER SAUCE
0.5 SUGAR
0.5 DARK SOY SAUCE

01
WASH AND DICE THE BEANS, MINCE THE GARLIC AND SET ASIDE
02
PUT OIL IN THE POT, PUT THE MINCED PORK AND STIR FRY, PUT ALL THE SAUCE INTO IT AND STIR FRY FOR 1 MINUTE.
03
IN A POT OF OIL, STIR FRY THE GARLIC, POUR IN THE BEAN CURD DICES AND STIR FRY FOR 3-5 MINUTES, YOU CAN ADD HALF A BOWL OF WATER TO SMOTHER.
04
ADD THE MEAT AND STIR FRY THE MEAT FOR 2 MINUTES.
05
PUT TWO SPOONS OF WATER IN THE BOWL, HALF A SPOONFUL OF CORNSTARCH, STIR WELL, POUR INTO THE POT, STIR FRY FOR 1-2 MINUTES, THICKENING CAN BE RELEASED.
