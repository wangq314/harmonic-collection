01
Pork slices add cooking wine, ginger, a little cornstarch, marinate for fifteen minutes.
02
When the oil is hot, add the beaten egg into the wok and scramble to the size of a thumb and set aside.
03
Put the marinated pork into the pan and stir-fry until golden brown, add bean paste, cooking wine, green and red chili peppers and garlic and stir-fry.
04
Lastly, pour in the eggs, add soy sauce, salt, sugar and chicken broth and stir fry
